1. Connecting the MFRC522 RFID Module to the ESP8266


MFRC522 RFID Module---->ESP8266 Wi -Fi Module

GND	                 GND
IRQ	                 NOT CONNECTED

SCK	                 D5 (GPIO14)
SDA	                 D4 (GPIO2)
