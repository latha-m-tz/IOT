
COMPONENST REQUIRED:

1)Arduino UNO

2)HC-05 Bluetooth Module


3)USB Cable (for powering the Arduino)

4)Breadboard and Jumper Wires (optional for easier connections)

WIRING INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Connecting HC-05 Bluetooth Module to Arduino UNO

HC-05 Pin	Arduino UNO Pin
TX	        Pin 5
RX	        Pin 6
VCC	        5V
GND	        GND
