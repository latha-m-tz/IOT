1)Arduino UNO

2)HM-10 Bluetooth Module

3)USB Cable (for powering the Arduino)

4)LED

WIRING INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Connecting HM-10 Bluetooth Module to Arduino UNO

HM-10 Pin	Arduino UNO Pin
TX	        Pin 2
RX	        Pin 3
VCC	        5V
GND	        GND

2.Connecting the led pin to the Arduino uno
