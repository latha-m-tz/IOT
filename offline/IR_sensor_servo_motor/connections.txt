COMPONENTS REQUIRED:

1)IR Sensor

2)Servo Motor

3)USB Cable (for powering the Arduino)

4)Jumper Wires
Male to female - 06
female to female -03

CONNECTIONS:

1. Connecting IR Sensor to Arduino UNO

IR Sensor Pin	Arduino UNO Pin

VCC	3.3V
GND	GND
OUT	Pin 8

2. Connecting Servo Motor to Arduino UNO

Servo Motor Pin	    Arduino UNO Pin
Red (Power)	      5V
Brown (GND)	      GND
Yellow (Signal)	      Pin 9
