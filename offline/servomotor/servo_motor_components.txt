COMPONENTS REQUIRED:

1)Servo Motor

2)Arduino UNO

3)USB Cable (for powering the Arduino)

4)Jumper Wires
male to male - 03

WIRING INSTRUCTIONS:
1.Connecting Servo Motor to Arduino UNO

Servo Motor Wire	                 Arduino UNO Pin

Red (Power)	                               5V
Black or Brown (Ground)	GND
Orange, White, or Yellow (Signal)	Digital Pin (e.g., Pin 9)
